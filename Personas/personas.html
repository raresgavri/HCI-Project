Emily realizes that she needs a solution to alleviate these issues and enhance her overall productivity.

She comes across an app specifically designed to assist individuals with visual impairments. This app offers features such as text resizing, a magnifier tool, and picture editing capabilities. Intrigued by its potential benefits, Emily decides to try it out. With the app, she can now easily adjust the text size on her smartphone or computer, allowing her to read and edit documents comfortably without straining her eyes. The magnifier tool helps her zoom in on small text or images, enabling her to comprehend and appreciate details that would otherwise be difficult to see.

The app's picture editing features also prove to be a valuable asset for Emily. She can now make quick edits and enhancements to images, giving her greater creative control and eliminating the need to rely on others for assistance. This newfound independence allows her to complete her marketing tasks efficiently and within her own timeline.

By utilizing such an app, Sarah hopes to alleviate the discomfort caused by conjunctivitis and regain her ability to perform daily tasks with greater ease and independence.
